Latching Technique

Ensure a deep latch by bringing the baby's chin to the breast first. Their mouth should cover a large portion of the areola, not just the nipple.

  • Belly-to-belly contact
  • Nose across from the nipple
  • Wide open mouth

Fluid Intelligence

Breast milk is over 80% water. Maintaining high hydration levels is critical for milk volume and your own energy recovery.

Daily Target

3.5 - 4.0 Liters

High iron content helps combat postpartum anemia and supports supply.
